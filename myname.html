Nickname Guesser

How Many Letters Are in Your Nickname?...

    How to Use

  1. Enter the number of letters in your nickname into the input field.
  2. Click the "Begin" button to generate a table.
  3. For each letter in your nickname, select the column containing that letter.
  4. After selecting all columns, the table will display rows with possible combinations of your nickname.
  5. Select the row containing each letter of your nickname in the correct order.
  6. Once all letters are selected, your nickname will be revealed!
